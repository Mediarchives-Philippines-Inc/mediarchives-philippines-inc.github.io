  • About Us

    MEDIARCHIVES PHILIPPINES INCORPORATION (MAPI), is a Philippine-based, accredited Teleradiology solutions provider offering medical imaging systems and solutions for both providers and recipients in the medical industry. As one of the pioneers in Southeast Asia promoting Teleradiology particularly in rural areas of the country, we average about 200 procedures/1000 exposure per day and read by Board certified group of radiologists within and outside of MAPI’s partner hospital sites.

    Partner sites include, but are not limited to, Bicol Regional Training Teaching Hospital (BRTTH) in Legazpi City, Bicol, Ospital ng Muntinlupa (OSMUN) and San Juan de Dios Hospital (SJDH) in Manila and Eversley Child Sanitarium Hospital (ECSH) in Mandaue city, Cebu. Medical images taken from these sites such as BRTTH and ECSH are read and diagnosed by a local team of radiologists as well as MAPI associate radiologists from Ospital ng Muntinlupa and San Juan de Dios Hospital in Manila.

    Our mission is to harness all medical images in all respective sites and to assure that a radiologist will be able to access and read in real-time regardless of location. Moreover, MAPI is continuously embarking its presence in remote areas in need of radiologists.

    The company was duly registered with the Security & Exchange Commission on August 28, 2008 with registration no. CS200813364. MAPI was registered in the Philippine Government Electronic Procurement System (Philgeps) with Certificate No. 2012-95111, issued in December of 2012. MAPI has complied with mandatory government requirements such as Mayor’s Permit, Barangay Clearance and BIR Tax Clearance; thus making MAPI a well-registered incorporation.

    The company’s purpose is to address the growing need of hospitals and clinics to improve it’s health systems and services without costly funding requirements. MAPI services focus on high quality, cost effective measures, and sharing technology in order to support continuing improvements in hospital care delivery.

    Joint venture agreements with partners such as BSI/Agfa Healthcare Systems, tasked with the service provisioning of its products, resulted in numerous site installations, decrease in operational costs and improved savings, and a significant increase in census and revenue in host sites and partner hospitals. We aim to cover more sites in the near future and increase the level of access to healthcare for doctors and practitioners in particularly underserved communities of the country.
